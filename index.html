Mace's Game Reviews

Here I have collected reviews and other info on games I've played over time. This started out with a top 100 games of all time list (accompanied with reviews), and then I started a "year in review" to collect new reviews of games as I play them. I've simplified things a bit and put all the reviews in one place. This serves as sort of an incomplete catalog of every game I've ever played - incomplete since it started in only 2023, although the back catalog of reviews I had created from the top 100 provides a pretty decent picture of my overall gaming history. There also won't be a review for everything I play at all, but I try to include anything that I put a decent amount of time into.

Lists:

Top 100 Games - List of top 100 games, determined by year
Years in Review - Games I played each year, starting 2023
Unreviewed Games - Stray game tracking

Reviews:

Main Reviews - All conventional releases I've written reviews for
Romhack Reviews - Reviews of ROMhacks I've played
